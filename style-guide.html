COLOUR PALETTE

The brands colour palette is based around multiple shades of dark and bright colours of grey and aqua which are contrasted with a light blue shade and complimented with a peach shade and pale yellow.

VISUAL MARQUE

Our visual marque is the dominant asset for the brand and will always be present in branding material. The crown represents me. As it might sound cliche and cheesy however my name 'Sarah' actually means princess in the Hebrew language so i wanted to add the element of the crown to portray that. Also i added my illustrative connection with the contrast of the pen illustration, therefore communicating the creative and fun aspects relating to the brand and its work.




MONOGRAM

The monogram is a combination of a two hand drawn letters. A Large Letter S and a smaller letter O. Which both represent my initials. I wanted to keep with the element of the visual marque so I wanted the brands monogram to have a joined up element and by doing so i wanted to include the enhance the curves that are situated with Both letters S and O. Allowing it to flows naturally. Its also very percise and clean design making it easily readable for consumers.


COLOUR VARIATIONS

The range of colours linked to the brand allows flexibility and therefore the monogram may be modified using the colours within the colour palette chosen. Other colours outside of this are not permitted.


    USAGE GUIDELINES:

  • Do not rotate
  • Do not change colour
  • Do not use the monogram in combination with the visual marque.
  • Do not change the direction of the monogram.
  • Do not add additional special effects
  • Do not animate visual marque or monogram


WORDMARK & TYPOGRAPHY

The brand uses a combination of two fonts Josefin sans and Open Sans, either may be used throughout the brand to serve different purposes. Both fonts come in a range of weights and styles, these styles may be used for headings, body, quotes and wherever typography is required. The simple sans serif typeface..... has been used for the brands wordmark and kept consistent throughout the brands material. The wordmark is postioned below and inline with the monogram

Both typefaces used are elegant and readable and reflects upon the brands clean, modern and elegant designs. The fonts are also appropriate in combination with the brands visual marque and monogram as the curved, soft edges are also carried across the brand identity. It is essential that the typefaces remains consistent through the entire brand. The typefaces proved should only be used.
